DO’s on Social Media:
- Maintain a professional and respectful online presence at all times
- Share meaningful, accurate, and constructive content
- Keep your profiles updated with relevant academic and professional information
- Protect your personal data and adjust privacy settings appropriately
- Think critically before posting, commenting, or sharing content

DON’Ts on Social Media:
- Do not share sensitive personal or financial information online
- Avoid posting offensive, inappropriate, or controversial content
- Do not engage in online arguments, harassment, or negative discussions
- Avoid spreading unverified information or misinformation
- Do not use informal, unprofessional language on public platforms
