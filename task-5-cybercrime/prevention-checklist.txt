Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips:

- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown attachments
- Do not share personal information publicly on social media
- Think carefully before posting or commenting online
- Do not engage in or encourage online harassment or bullying
- Block and report users who engage in abusive behavior
- Be cautious while interacting with unknown people online

Financial/UPI Safety:
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone
- Do not approve payment requests unless you have verified the source

Report Cybercrime:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline: 1930
